How to check Blood Pressure

  1. Sit in a chair with both feet flat on the ground and your back straight. It is recommended you sit for about 5 minutes before measuring your blood pressure.
  2. Rest your arm on a surface (such as a table) around the level of your heart or chest.
  3. Take your blood pressure according to the instructions of your machine. Stay still and do not talk as the machine is working.
  4. It can sometimes be helpful to repeat another blood pressure reading 1 to 2 minutes later and use the average of the 2 readings.
